Paste a Warcraft Logs report and analyze it: flasks, elixirs, potions, Flame Caps, Nightmare Seeds, runes, bombs & sapper charges, and scrolls (Agility V / Strength V) are counted per player and priced with TSM data.

Custom consumables (advanced)

Track anything not in the built-in list — just like the built-ins, detection is automatic per log: if the report contains a buff with this exact name, usage is estimated from buff uptime (names starting with "Flask" count as 120 min & death-persistent, "Scroll" as 30 min, everything else as 60 min); otherwise casts with this exact ability name are counted. Use the name exactly as shown in Warcraft Logs. TSM item ID = the number in the item's Wowhead URL (used for pricing — hit "Update TSM prices now" after adding).
